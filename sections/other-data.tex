\subsection{Math mode}
Example:
\begin{equation}
a + b = c + d
\end{equation}
Aligning:
\begin{align*}
	a &= 5 \\
	b + c &= a \\
	a -2*3 &= 5/4
\end{align*}
Hint: Variables or equations in text are separated with \$ sign, e.g. $a$, $x - y$.
